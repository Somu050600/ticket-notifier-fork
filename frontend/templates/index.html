🔑 Transfer Bot Session to Your Browser

  1. Install Cookie-Editor (Chrome/Edge/Firefox) or EditThisCookie.
  2. Tap 📋 Copy Session in the alert above — this copies the bot's wristband to your clipboard.
  3. Open the cart link in a new tab (it'll bounce you to /cinemas — that's fine).
  4. Click the Cookie-Editor icon → Import → paste → Save.
  5. Refresh the page. You now hold the bot's session — go straight to checkout.
Cookies expire quickly, so paste and pay fast. If the page still bounces, tap Copy Session again — the bot may have updated the wristband.
